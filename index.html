Dental services

Healthy teeth - healthy body

Braces

It involves the correction of mal-aligned teeth, protruded teeth , crowded teeth , etc by the use of braces or clear aligners. it helps achieve a pleasant and beautiful smile.

Crowns bridges & Veneers

By restoring your old teeth , crowns and veneers help improve function and occlusion along with gifting you a wonderful smile

implants

Dental implants may be an option for people who have lost a tooth or teeth due to periodontal disease, an injury, or some other reason.

Extractions

In orthodontics if the teeth are crowded, sound teeth may be extracted to create space so the rest of the teeth can be straightened.
